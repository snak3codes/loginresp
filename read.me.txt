Day Stop App

An organizational app to track and organize all your daily activities!! Add your todos to the todo app, add your activities to the interactive map 
app, so you will never miss a single event! 

Usage notes
----------------------------------------
Open index.html in a browser.
Choose an activity, pick a time. The marker will be displayed to your map at the location you want. 

Open index1.html in a browser.
Add a todo, track your todos. Delete or Hide your todos.

Notes
----------------------------------------
This program does not require installation. 
This program requires a browser
